PolyXpress PointFinder

Instructions

PolyXpress PointFinder is a field tool that allows PolyXpress authors to accurately capture GPS location points for their stories. The tool includes an accuracy test to get detailed information on how accurate the geolocation information is allowing authors to pick appropriate event ranges. Last, the author can save points of interest along with short notes for later use.

Be sure to "start tracking" before capturing points.
