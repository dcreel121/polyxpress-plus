PolyXpress Player

The PolyXpress player is a web application that allows you to experience location-based stories on all of your iOS and android devices. It takes you on a journey through real-world locations where you interact with your environment as a multimedia story unfolds.

PolyXpress Mobile Designer

PolyXpress Mobile Designer allows you to create PolyXpress, location-based stories. Once you have everything just right, you can publish your story and share it with PolyXpress users around the world. (The full desktop browser designer tool will be released soon.)

PolyXpress PointFinder

This field tool provides the means to determine the GPS coordinates for your present location and also associate short notes. As you build your story, it will help you record points of interest.
